Frequently Asked Questions

How does Clearoid detect duplicates?

Clearoid uses AI-powered semantic similarity analysis to detect duplicates based on meaning, not just exact text matches.

What file formats are supported?

We support Excel (.xlsx, .xls) and CSV files for bulk uploads.

How do I export my data?

Navigate to the Export page and click the Download button to get your clean data as an Excel file.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard security measures and do not share your data with third parties.

How do I report an issue?

Email us at support@clearoid.ai with details about the issue.
